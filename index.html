Leave your footprints in the sand as you circle the island. Blue skies, summer days all year round… it’s the kind of place that will refresh you… it’s where doing nothing has become an art form.

Where We Are

Situated on a picturesque marine sanctuary in the heart of the Mamanuca Islands, Beachcomber Island Resort is just 19kms from Nadi International airport.

It’s an island that’s easy to get to yet so very hard to leave! Beachcomber Island is the perfect island destination in Fiji. It is unique, spectacular and fun. If you love a great time in the sun with great company in a casual, relaxed atmosphere, then this is the place for you.

It is perfect for those looking for fun in the sun, a bit of nightlife and extensive marine activities.

How To Get Here

Transfers to Beachcomber Island. We operate our own fast and affordable transfer service from Anchorage private jetty located at Vuda Marina, for guests travelling to Beachcomber Island Resort.

We have pickups from Nadi Airport and Port Denarau where you will be greeted with shell leis and personally accompanied by our own staff to meet our marine transfer service.
